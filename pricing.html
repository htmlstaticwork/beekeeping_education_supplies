No, live bees (packages or nucs) are sold separately due to seasonal agricultural shipping restrictions. However, purchasing the bundle gives you priority access to reserve our spring bee packages.
Can I cancel my digital membership at any time?
Yes, our monthly subscriptions can be canceled at any time from your dashboard settings. You will retain access until the end of your billing cycle.
