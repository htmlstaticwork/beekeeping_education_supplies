Educational Workshops

Interactive online and in-person courses designed to take you from a curious beginner to a confident apiarist.

Comprehensive Beekeeping Education

Our educational workshops are meticulously structured to cover the complete lifecycle of a honeybee colony. We provide both asynchronous video courses for remote learning, as well as hands-on field days in our teaching apiaries.

Led by master beekeepers, these sessions blend scientific entomology with practical, time-tested hive management techniques. You will learn to properly inspect frames, identify diseases early, and safely harvest honey.

What You'll Learn

Core competencies covered in our specialized programs.

Disease Identification

Recognize the signs of Varroa mites, American Foulbrood, and Chalkbrood before they devastate the colony.

Nectar Flows

Understand local flora bloom times and how to time your supering for maximum honey yield.

Overwintering & Prep

Prepare your hives to survive harsh winters by managing moisture and ensuring adequate stores.

Swarm Prevention

Learn advanced splitting techniques and population management to prevent costly swarming.
