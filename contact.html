Support & FAQ

Find quick answers to common questions about our services and supplies.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days. Express options are available at checkout for urgent beekeeping supplies.

Do you offer on-site consulting?

Yes! We provide on-site apiary assessments within the Austin area. For customers further away, we offer virtual video consultations.

Can I return educational courses?

Online courses are eligible for a full refund within 14 days of purchase if less than 20% of the content has been viewed.

Are the supplies professional grade?

Absolutely. All our equipment is tested in our own commercial apiaries to ensure it meets the highest standards for durability and safety.
