What You Will Learn

  • The lifecycle and caste system of a honey bee colony.
  • How to choose the best location for your apiary.
  • Step-by-step hive assembly and bee installation.
  • Proper protective clothing and smoker usage.
  • Seasonal management tasks (Spring, Summer, Fall).
  • Recognizing and treating common bee diseases.

Requirements

  • No prior beekeeping experience required.
  • A passion for nature and sustainability.
  • Basic protective gear (recommended for lesson 3 onwards).
